# TWS Trend Intelligence V2.0.3



A powerful all-in-one market intelligence dashboard designed for traders who want trend clarity, market context, volatility awareness, and actionable insights in a single view.



## Features



• FRAMA Trend Engine

Detects the dominant market trend using a Fractal Adaptive Moving Average.



• SSL Hybrid Confirmation

Provides bullish and bearish trend confirmation while reducing market noise.



• ADX Strength Analysis

Measures trend strength and classifies conditions as LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, or EXTREME.



• Choppiness Detection (CHOP)

Identifies trending and sideways market conditions to help avoid low-quality setups.



• ATR Room Meter

Displays how much of the day's Average True Range (ATR) has already been consumed.



• ATR Left Projection

Shows the estimated remaining ATR expansion potential based on the previous day's closing price.



• Gap Intelligence Engine

Analyzes Gap-Up and Gap-Down openings and provides contextual market guidance during the opening phase.



• Smart Entry Plan Engine

Generates adaptive market guidance such as:



* Trade Ready Long

* Trade Ready Short

* Look For Long

* Look For Short

* Wait Breakout



• Dynamic Action Engine

Automatically shifts based on market progression:



* Buy Ready

* Sell Ready

* Trail Profits

* Book Profits



• Volume Intelligence

Tracks buy-side and sell-side participation to help traders understand market pressure and momentum.



• Market Mood Engine

Classifies market behavior into:



* Bullish Control

* Bearish Control

* Bullish Expansion

* Bearish Expansion

* Sideways Compression



• Trade Quality Rating

Grades setups based on overall market conditions:



* A+

* A

* B

* Avoid



• Multi-Timeframe Alignment Matrix

Monitor trend alignment across user-configurable timeframes and identify high-conviction opportunities.



## Suitable For

✔ Swing Trading

# TWS Beast Mode Trend Matrix

## Overview

TWS Beast Mode Trend Matrix is a multi-layer trend analysis system designed to help traders identify trend direction, trend quality, market structure, liquidity targets, volatility conditions, and multi-timeframe alignment from a single dashboard.

The indicator combines FRAMA, SSL Hybrid, ADX, ATR, CHOP, VWAP, Volume Analysis, Market Regime Detection, Institutional Flow Analysis, Liquidity Mapping, and MTF Alignment into one decision-support framework.

It is built for traders who prefer clean charts and objective market analysis rather than relying on traditional buy/sell signal indicators.

---

## Key Features

### Trader Mode Profiles

The indicator automatically adapts its internal settings based on trading style:

* Normal Mode
* Intraday Mode
* Scalper Mode
* Swing Mode

Each profile automatically adjusts trend sensitivity and filtering logic.

Dashboard Profile Display:

* F34 | S10 | A25 (Intraday)
* F21 | S5 | A20 (Scalper)
* F55 | S20 | A30 (Swing)

---

### Trend Engine

Uses FRAMA and SSL Hybrid alignment to classify market structure:

* Strong Long
* Long
* Strong Short
* Short
* Sideways

Designed to reduce noise and avoid low-quality trend conditions.

---

### Market Strength Analysis

Combines:

* ADX
* ATR Expansion
* CHOP Index

To determine:

* Trend Strength
* Market Condition
* Risk Environment

---

### Multi-Timeframe Matrix

Monitors trend alignment across multiple user-selected timeframes.

Provides:

* Alignment Score
* Trend Direction Per Timeframe
* Overall Trend Consensus

Useful for confirmation before entering trades.

---

### Liquidity Target Engine

Automatically identifies likely liquidity destinations:

* PDH (Previous Day High)
* PDL (Previous Day Low)
* SH (Swing High)
* SL (Swing Low)
* WH (Weekly High)
* WL (Weekly Low)

Dashboard examples:

* PDH@25184
* PDL@24952
* SH@25240
* WL@24320

---

### ATR Room Engine

Tracks how much of the current ATR range has already been consumed.

Provides:

* ATR Usage %
* ATR Remaining %
* Daily ATR Projection

Useful for avoiding late entries when most of the expected move is already completed.

---

### Institutional & Market Regime Engines

Analyzes:

* Institutional Participation
* Smart Money Bias
* Market Regime Conditions
* Probability Framework

Helps distinguish trending markets from inefficient or low-conviction environments.

---

### Dashboard System

Desktop and Mobile dashboards include:

* Trader Mode
* Trend Bias
* ADX Strength
* ATR Status
* CHOP Condition
* VWAP Bias
* Volume Strength
* Liquidity Targets
* Institutional Confidence
* Smart Money Flow
* Market Regime
* Market Score

All major decision metrics are available without cluttering the chart.

---

## Best Use Cases

* Index Trading
* Futures Trading
* Options Trading
* Intraday Trading
* Scalping
* Swing Trading
* Trend Following Strategies

## Notes

This indicator is designed as a market analysis and decision-support tool. It is not intended to provide guaranteed trading signals or financial advice. Always use proper risk management and confirm setups with your own trading plan.
