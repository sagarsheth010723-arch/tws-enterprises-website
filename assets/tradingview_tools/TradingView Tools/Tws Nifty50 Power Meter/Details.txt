TWS Nifty50 Power Meter

A real-time Nifty breadth and strength dashboard designed to track participation across major Nifty constituents. The indicator provides a live stock grid, bullish/bearish participation, market mode detection, and a proprietary Power Score to help traders assess overall market strength at a glance.
