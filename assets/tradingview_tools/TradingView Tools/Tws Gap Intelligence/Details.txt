TWS Gap Intelligence Engine is a gap-based market analysis tool designed to evaluate the quality, strength, and potential outcome of opening gaps.

The indicator combines multiple analytical engines including Gap Classification, Gap Acceptance, Liquidity Void Detection, Trap Absorption Analysis, Market State Evaluation, Target Projection, Closing Pressure Assessment, and Next Day Forecasting.

Key Features:
• Gap Type Classification (Gap Up, Gap Down, Flat Open)
• Gap Strength Assessment
• Real vs Fake Gap Detection
• Gap Fill Probability Analysis
• Market State Identification
• Projection-Based Target Calculation
• Intraday Bias Evaluation
• Next Day Opening Forecast
• Clean Dashboard-Based Interface

The objective of this indicator is not to generate buy or sell signals, but to provide a structured framework for understanding how the market is reacting to an opening gap and what outcomes are statistically more likely.

All projections and forecasts are derived from price action, volatility, momentum, and internal market structure analysis.

This tool is intended for traders who want additional context around gap behavior, market acceptance, continuation potential, and forecasted market conditions.
