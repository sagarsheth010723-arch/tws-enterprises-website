About TWS

Built from practical market experience and a long-term value creation mindset.

TWS Enterprises Private Limited is a financial market research and advisory-focused company founded by Mr. Sagar H. Sheth, who began his journey in the financial markets in 2012.

He launched TradesWithSaagar in 2016 with the vision of helping retail traders and investors achieve long-term financial growth through disciplined, research-based strategies. In 2024, TradesWithSaagar was officially incorporated as TWS Enterprises Private Limited.

VisionTo become one of India's most trusted financial market companies.
Strength12+ years market experience, research mindset and strong capital-market legacy.
