TWS Nifty50 Power Meter

TWS Nifty50 Power Meter is a market-strength and sentiment-analysis indicator developed specifically to assess the internal strength of the Nifty 50 and the broader Indian equity market.

Request Details
DESCRIPTION

Understand the market with better context.

TWS Nifty50 Power Meter is a market-strength and sentiment-analysis indicator developed specifically to assess the internal strength of the Nifty 50 and the broader Indian equity market.

The indicator helps traders understand whether bullish or bearish pressure is dominating and whether the index movement is supported by meaningful market participation.

It provides an additional perspective on overall market strength, participation, directional pressure and sentiment before a trader considers the intended direction.

TWS TradingView Tools are market-analysis and educational tools only. They do not provide guaranteed Buy or Sell recommendations, assured returns or guaranteed trading outcomes. Users must conduct independent analysis and follow appropriate risk management.

Suitable For

  • Nifty 50 traders
  • Bank Nifty and index traders
  • Indian stock-market traders
  • Intraday index-option traders
  • Futures and options traders
  • Traders using broader-market confirmation
  • Traders assessing bullish and bearish pressure

Key Benefits

  • Measures overall Nifty 50 market strength
  • Evaluates bullish and bearish pressure
  • Identifies dominant market sentiment
  • Provides broader-market confirmation for index trades
  • Supports market timing and directional assessment
  • Assesses whether a move has sufficient participation
  • Supports stock-selection context
  • Helps avoid positions conflicting with prevailing market strength
