TWS TradingView Tools

Structured market information for disciplined decision-making.

TWS TradingView Tools help evaluate trend quality, momentum, participation, price structure, liquidity behaviour, volatility and opening-session conditions. They do not promise guaranteed returns and should be used with risk management and independent analysis.

Five Analytical Tools

Each tool serves a different analytical purpose.

Use them independently or as part of a broader trading process.

Trend Analysis

TWS Trend Intelligence

TWS Trend Intelligence is an advanced trend-analysis indicator designed to help traders identify the prevailing market direction, evaluate trend strength and understand the quality of price momentum.

Explore Tool →
Market Strength & Sentiment

TWS Nifty50 Power Meter

TWS Nifty50 Power Meter is a market-strength and sentiment-analysis indicator developed specifically to assess the internal strength of the Nifty 50 and the broader Indian equity market.

Explore Tool →
Smart Money Concepts

TWS SMC Apex

TWS SMC Apex is an advanced price-action and market-structure indicator based on Smart Money Concepts.

Explore Tool →
Market Diagnostics & Trade Quality

TWS Market Vitals

TWS Market Vitals is a dual-dashboard market-diagnostics and trade-quality indicator designed to evaluate the overall condition of the market before a trader considers an entry.

Explore Tool →
Opening-Session Analysis

TWS Gap Intelligence

TWS Gap Intelligence is an opening-session analysis indicator designed to study gap-up, gap-down, flat and near-flat market openings.
